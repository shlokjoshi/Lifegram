Designing stories.

Themesberg was born in 2018 when our founders looked at the top digital agencies and saw that something was missing. Something essential.

Where it all began

Themesberg was founded by two friends who decided to bring together their experience in marketing, design and technology to create their view of the ideal agency. Taking all the best bits we learned from big firms, we’ve refined and implemented robust ways of working into our studio.
